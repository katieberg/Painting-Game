Nonogram - A Puzzle Game

The object of a picross puzzle is to darken all the correct squares in the grid to reveal a hidden image. The numbers on the sides of each row and column represent continuous blocks of darkened cells. Note that every block of darkened cells must be seperated by at least one blank cell.
